Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a parking slot? ▾
Go to the Dashboard and click "Book Slot". Select your preferred parking area, choose a date and time, then confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation notification instantly.
How do I cancel a booking? ▾
Go to My Bookings, find the booking you want to cancel, and click the "Cancel" button. Cancellations made more than 2 hours before your slot are fully refunded.
How do I update my vehicle information? ▾
Open Profile Settings from the top-right menu and update your vehicle information under the "Vehicle Details" section. Changes are saved immediately.
Who do I contact for payment issues? ▾
Use the contact form on this page or email us at support@findmyparking.com. For urgent payment issues, call our helpline at +91 98765 43210. We respond within 2 hours on business days.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay). All payments are secured and encrypted.
How long does a refund take? ▾
Refunds are processed within 24 hours of cancellation and typically reflect in your account within 3–5 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.
